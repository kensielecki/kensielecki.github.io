Revenue

DeFi uses DefiLlama Protocol Revenue — the protocol's cut after paying liquidity providers, annualised from a 30-day rolling window. Equity uses Yahoo Finance Total Revenue TTM. Same column in the table, different denominators — documented in the methodology.

Earnings & P/E

DeFi Earnings = Protocol Revenue minus token incentives (the equivalent of customer acquisition cost). This is what DefiLlama calls "earnings" and what Token Terminal uses for P/E. Identical formula to equity net income divided by market cap.

Div / Holder Yield

DeFi: annualised holder revenue divided by market cap — includes fee buybacks, burns, and direct staking distributions. Hyperliquid's 8% is a buyback yield; GMX's 17% is paid in ETH. Equity: trailing 12-month cash dividend yield from Yahoo Finance.

02 — Tools & Automation
02 — 2026
Automation

SF/Bay Area Crypto Events Aggregator

In crypto, most deals start with a conversation at an event. This tool scrapes Luma, Eventbrite, and Telegram channels for crypto, DeFi, and AI events across the Bay Area, runs a two-tier relevance filter to remove noise, then uses Claude to re-rank by quality and fit.

Runs weekly via launchd. Output delivered as a ranked digest to Telegram — no manual trawling through 8+ event sites. Saves roughly 2 hours of research per week.

Telegram BD Automation (tg-blast)

Most crypto BD happens on Telegram. This tool handles the operational layer: bulk messaging to segmented contact lists, channel broadcasting, and delivery tracking — all through a password-protected local web UI so outreach can be managed without touching code.

Built for conference follow-up, partner outreach, and deal flow broadcasting. Operates on top of the Telegram MTProto API via Telethon so it uses your actual account, not a bot — messages land in DMs, not a channel from an unknown sender.

04 — 2026
DeFi Analytics

DeFi Yield Dashboard & Alerts

Monitors lending rates and yield opportunities across DeFi via DefiLlama. Diffs daily snapshots and fires Telegram alerts when yields shift beyond a configurable threshold — so significant moves in Aave, Morpho, or Pendle don't go unnoticed.

The core metric tracked is the carry spread between on-chain yield and the US 10-year Treasury. When that spread compresses below a threshold, the risk-adjusted case for DeFi yield weakens. Built on the same data pipeline as the valuation screener.
